Our Mission

We want to remove the stigma around sanitary pads and bring them out of the black plastic / brown paper bag environment for which we put the product front-and-center of the retail-environment where actual transactions happen. There are still a lot of people who are uncomfortable handling pads but the advent of modern retail formats have helped in a huge way to tackle this barrier.

1 out of 5 girls in the country are dropping out of school when they get their 1st period because they have no prior knowledge about it so it is also Important to teach them the same.

India has come a long way, but there is still quite an uphill journey left. We will continue to be committed to unleashing a girl’s confidence and making her an unstoppable force.
