OUR MISSION

The menstrual hygiene category in India has come leaps and bounds from where it was when Anjali Sanitary Pads got the vision.

Periods was something no one talked about openly and advertising about menstrual hygiene products was unheard of. Since then, Indian women have changed from being almost completely cloth users to about 50% of them (in urban areas) now using pads. Over the next 20 years, we are focusing on two things: awareness and access. We want every woman in the country to know the importance of good menstrual hygiene.

Taboos, lack of information and access to sanitary napkins are just some of the reasons why menstruation still limits the lives of women in many places. We believe that together we can make a difference and for that is leading a series of initiatives to transform the reality of those who need it most.
